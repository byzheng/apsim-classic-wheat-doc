APSIM Classic Wheat Documentation

2 APSIM-Wheat history

APSIM-Wheat has been developed from a combination of the approaches used in previous APSIM wheat modules: Asseng, Keating, et al. (1998); Asseng, Fillery, et al. (1998); Keating et al. (2003); Meinke et al. (1997); Meinke et al. (1998). The current version of the model is implemented within the APSIM Plant model framework which is currently used for other crops such as grain legumes and canola. Most of the model constants (species-specific) and parameters (cultivar specific) are externalized from the code (wheat.xml file).
